Install MTC Court

To use MTC Court, add it to your home screen first. It takes 10 seconds!

1
Tap Share Bottom of screen (iPhone) or top-right (iPad)
2
Tap "Add to Home Screen" Scroll down if you don’t see it
3
Tap "Add" MTC Court will appear on your home screen
After installing, open the MTC Court app from your home screen to sign in.
